About Me

My name is Rifhanis Irdina Binti Resman and this year on 1/11/2025 I will turn 21 years old. Twenty-one years such a big number in my life. This year, I will no longer be swayed by fleeting impulses, but will act with purpose and wisdom, knowing that every decision shapes my future.

I am incredibly grateful for the love and support of my family, who have always been my foundation. Especially my mother and father, who have always been my reason to keep going in this life. Their ambition and hard work inspire me daily to pursue my own dreams with passion and determination.

I am deeply grateful for the bond I share with my siblings, who have always been my strongest supporters. Together, we inspire each other to grow, overcome challenges, and aim for success. Our shared vision of creating a better future fuels our determination, and I’m thankful for the unwavering love and encouragement we give one another. With them by my side, I’m excited for the journey ahead.
